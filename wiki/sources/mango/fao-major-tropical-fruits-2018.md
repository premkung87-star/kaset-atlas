## English summary

FAO 2018 statistical compendium for major tropical fruits. Provides world-level production and trade context for mango, including its share among major tropical fruit crops. Tier-1 international reference; used in the Kaset Atlas profile to frame the global picture into which Thai exports fit.

## Notable citable facts

- Mango is the dominant volume category among the FAO "major tropical fruits" group.
- Trade flows shown at country and region level for the 2018 reference period.
- FAO reports rely on national statistics offices; figures should be read as the most-recent-available rather than real-time.
