## English summary

Thai academic-journal analysis of Thai mango exports — markets, phytosanitary requirements (notably the Japan VHT pathway), and the structural challenges of scaling export volume. Used as a Tier-1 Thai-context complement to the DOAE export advisory.

## Notable citable facts

- Japan is consistently named as a notable export market dependent on the VHT protocol.
- Export volume has historically grown slowly relative to domestic consumption.
- Phytosanitary compliance is a recurring constraint across Asian destination markets.
