## English summary

UF/IFAS Mango Science phenology page for the Mahachanok cultivar. Documents fruit characteristics and developmental phenology of the Thai-developed hybrid. Used in the mango profile as a corroborating international source for Mahachanok cultivar facts.

## Notable citable facts

- Mahachanok fruit weight in the 365–410 g range.
- Fiber-free flesh, yellowish peel with pinkish-red blush distal end.
- Thai hybrid; corroborates the Wikipedia parentage attribution (Sunset × Nang Klanwan).
