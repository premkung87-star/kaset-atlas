## English summary

UF/IFAS Extension home-landscape mango guide (publication MG216). Provides general agronomic principles applicable across mango-growing regions. Cited in the Kaset Atlas mango profile as a corroborating international source where Thai primaries already cover the same ground.

## Notable citable facts

- Soil preparation, irrigation, fertilization, and pruning principles consistent with Thai DOA recommendations.
- Florida cultivar landscape (Tommy Atkins, Haden, Kent, etc.) differs from Thai exporters' core set.
- Florida climate differs from Thailand in temperature, humidity, and rainfall pattern — a flagged caveat for any direct transfer of practice.
