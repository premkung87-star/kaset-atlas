# Safety Policy — Kaset Atlas

> Bad agricultural advice can harm crops, money, livelihoods, and people. Kaset Atlas refuses overconfident advice in high-risk areas.

---

## 1. Refusal Categories

Kaset Atlas **does not publish** specific advice on:

### Chemical Use
- ❌ Pesticide dosages or mixing ratios
- ❌ Herbicide application schedules
- ❌ Foliar fertilizer concentrations for specific products
- ❌ Tank mix recommendations
- ❌ Re-entry intervals or pre-harvest intervals (PHI)

**Reason:** Wrong chemical advice causes crop loss, environmental damage, human poisoning, and legal violations.

**What we do instead:** Explain general principles (e.g., "ใช้สารชีวภัณฑ์เป็นทางเลือกแรกก่อนสารเคมี") and direct readers to product labels, agricultural extension officers, and the Department of Agriculture.

### Plant Identification for Edibility
- ❌ "Is this plant safe to eat?"
- ❌ Wild mushroom identification
- ❌ Foraging guides for similar-looking edible/poisonous plants
- ❌ Medicinal dosage recommendations

**Reason:** Misidentification can cause serious illness or death.

**What we do instead:** Provide cultivar identification for known cultivated plants only, with explicit warnings against using the information for foraging.

### Medical / Health Claims
- ❌ "พืชนี้รักษาโรค X ได้"
- ❌ Dosage recommendations for medicinal herbs
- ❌ Drug interaction information
- ❌ Substitution for prescribed medication

**Reason:** Not licensed medical professionals; risk of harm.

**What we do instead:** Cite traditional uses with appropriate caveats ("ตามตำรายาไทยมีการใช้... แต่ควรปรึกษาแพทย์ก่อน").

### Profit / Yield Guarantees
- ❌ "ปลูกพืช X ได้กำไรเดือนละ Y บาท"
- ❌ Specific market price predictions
- ❌ ROI calculations presented as reliable
- ❌ "Zero-risk" investment claims

**Reason:** Markets are volatile; outcomes vary by region, scale, market access, and skill.

**What we do instead:** Provide ranges, cite source data, and explicitly note variability.

### Disease Diagnosis from Image
- ❌ Confirming plant disease from photo alone
- ❌ Insect identification for pest control decisions

**Reason:** Misdiagnosis leads to wrong treatment.

**What we do instead:** Describe symptoms, list possible causes, and recommend consultation with extension officers or laboratory diagnosis.

## 2. Required Safety Language

For content that touches risk-adjacent topics, include warning boxes with this Thai language:

```
⚠️ ควรตรวจสอบกับผู้เชี่ยวชาญในพื้นที่ก่อนใช้สารเคมี

⚠️ ข้อมูลนี้เป็นแนวทางทั่วไป ไม่ใช่คำแนะนำเฉพาะแปลง

⚠️ ควรอ่านฉลากผลิตภัณฑ์และปฏิบัติตามกฎหมายไทย

⚠️ ไม่ควรยืนยันชนิดพืชที่กินได้จากภาพเพียงอย่างเดียว

⚠️ ข้อมูลด้านสุขภาพควรปรึกษาแพทย์หรือเภสัชกรก่อนใช้
```

## 3. Risk-Adjacent Content Workflow

When writing content that touches restricted areas:

1. **Identify the risk** — Does this section approach a refusal category?
2. **Switch to general principles** — Talk about *why* and *what to consider*, not *exactly how much*
3. **Add safety box** — Use one of the standard warnings above
4. **Direct to authority** — Link to กรมวิชาการเกษตร, agricultural extension, product labels, etc.
5. **Get reviewer if available** — Mark as `reviewer-needed` in frontmatter

---

## 4. AI-Generated Content Rules

- AI may help draft, summarize, translate, and structure content
- **No AI-generated content is published without human review**
- Human reviewer must verify every source citation
- Human reviewer must verify every claim against the cited source
- AI cannot be cited as a source

Rule:

> AI can assist, but sources decide.

---

## 5. Sponsorship & Conflict of Interest

- Sponsored content must be clearly labeled
- Sponsors cannot influence technical recommendations
- No undisclosed product placement
- Maintainer financial interests must be declared in sponsored content
- Currently: **No sponsorships accepted.** Project is funded by maintainer's other work.

## 6. Reader Reporting

Readers can report safety concerns via:

- GitHub Issues with `safety` label
- Email (when configured)

All safety reports are reviewed within 7 days.
